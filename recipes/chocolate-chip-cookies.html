Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Try this scrumptious classic chocolate chip cookie recipe now! Gooey on the inside, and crispy on the outside, just how it should be.

  • Prep Time: 20 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 30 mins
  • Servings: 48
  • Yield: 4 dozen

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl
  3. Drop 2 spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto baking sheet
  4. Bake until edges are golden brown
